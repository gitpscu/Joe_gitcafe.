Signature Latte Recipe

Ingredients:
- 1 cup of freshly brewed espresso
- 1 cup of steamed milk
- 1 tablespoon of vanilla syrup
- A sprinkle of cinnamon (optional)
- A drizzle of caramel sauce (optional)

Instructions:
1. Brew a fresh cup of espresso and pour it into a coffee cup.
2. Steam the milk until it's frothy and add it to the espresso.
3. Stir in the vanilla syrup to blend the flavors.
4. For added flavor, sprinkle a bit of cinnamon on top and drizzle with caramel sauce if desired.
5. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious signature latte!

Note: You can adjust the sweetness and flavors according to your preference.
